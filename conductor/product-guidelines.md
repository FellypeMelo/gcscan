# GCScan - Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Formal & Technical:** Use precise, academic, and serious language. Documentation and interface labels should follow standard bioinformatics terminology.

## Branding & Aesthetic
- **Scientific & Clean:** The visual identity should be professional, utilizing blue and green tones to evoke a scientific and trustworthy feel.

## UX Principles
- **Efficiency & Speed:** Optimize workflows for power users, ensuring that common tasks (like batch processing) are fast and require minimal interaction.
- **Guided & Error-proof:** Provide helpful hints, clear validation messages, and guardrails to prevent user errors, especially during data entry and configuration.
- **Visual Clarity:** Prioritize the readability and aesthetics of data visualizations (charts, histograms) to ensure that genomic insights are easy to interpret.

## Localization & Internationalization
- **Multi-language Support:** Support both Portuguese (PT-BR) and English (Global) to ensure accessibility for both local and international research communities.
