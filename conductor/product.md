# GCScan - Product Guide

## Mission
To provide a reliable, efficient, and accessible tool for bioinformaticians, researchers, and students to analyze GC content in DNA sequences, facilitating genomic analysis and quality control.

## Target Users
- **Researchers/Scientists:** Analyzing genomic data for research purposes.
- **Students/Educators:** Learning and teaching DNA analysis and bioinformatics.
- **Data Pipelines/DevOps:** Integrating GC analysis into automated workflows.

## Primary Goals
- **Sequence Quality Control:** Rapidly assess the quality of sequencing data.
- **Taxonomic/Genomic Analysis:** Assist in identifying species or specific genomic regions.
- **Automation & Scalability:** Support high-throughput processing of large genomic datasets.

## Key Features
- **Sliding Window Analysis:** Analyze local GC content variations across sequences.
- **CpG Island Detection:** Identify potential regulatory regions (CpG islands).
- **Professional Reporting:** Generate comprehensive reports in PDF and Excel formats.
- **Visual Dashboard:** Interactive visualization of GC statistics.

## Success Metrics
- **Performance & Speed:** Efficiently handle massive FASTA files.
- **Accuracy & Reliability:** Ensure precise and verified calculations.
- **User Accessibility:** Maintain an intuitive interface for both CLI and Web users.
